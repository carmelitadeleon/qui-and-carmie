Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is the RSVP Deadline?

A: August 30th is the last day to RSVP.

Q: Can I bring a plus one?

A: Please contact the bride or groom if you can bring one.

Q: Are kids allowed?

A: Yes, kids are allowed if the wedding invite (not Save the Date) includes "name and family". If it does not, please ask the bride or groom. Please include their names when you RSVP for food headcount purposes.



Q: Speaking of food, what if I have food allergies?

A: Please mention your food allergies in the RSVP form in the comments.



Also speaking of food, is it plated or a buffet?


A: Dinner will be served buffet style!!



Q: What should I wear?

A: The dress code is semi-formal/formal. *Bridal party will be wearing lilac/dark purple. The bride will be wearing white.



Q: Is the wedding indoors/outdoors?

A: The ceremony and the reception will take place indoors.



Q: Can we take photos/videos during your wedding?

A: Of course!! Just please be respectful of the photographer/videographer (be mindful of their space) and be discreet during the church wedding ceremony.



Q: How is parking at the church/reception?

A: Parking is free at the church and has two floors. Parking is also free at the reception and it has two floors as well! Plenty of parking!
